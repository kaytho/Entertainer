Earl D. Thomas(EDT), founder of MyNameIsReallyEarl, Inc is a born leader and Texas native who operates in three entitites: theManakin, theMime, and theMovement. Through these different areas, EDT seeks to encourage others worldwide to become passionate about the inner gifts the possess. Pursuing his first true love of action, EDT has starred in numerous stage productions throughout DFW including Divinity Productions's stage plays, A Merry Mystery and The Rebirth of Christmas, Kizzy Fore's production, Scorned, and GTOD Productions;s Risen. He was also cast in Season 1 of the reality show, The Moguls Dallas. In addition to acting EDT hosts a variety of events in fashion, fine arts and outreach including ByDesign's Poetry and Soul, Bring it To The Mic, Miss Aspiration Pageant, and the RIP the Runway Gospel Fest. EDT, an ordained minister, a dverse artist, and author , shares some of his life experiences on overcoming adversity through the three principles of Fearless, Focused, Faithful in his upcoming book entitled An Educated Recover. With God on his side, a sturdy foundation, and great family support, EDT knows that when you discover the passions that drive you, your life will be nothing short of greatness. Follow EDT on this journey and experience theManakin, theMime, theMovement!
